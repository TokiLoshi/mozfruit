MozFruit - A Sustainable Oasis in the Heart of Chigamane

At MozFruit, we pride ourselves on sustainable and innovative farming practices, producing a bountiful harvest of dragon fruit and a diverse array of other delicious fruits and vegetables. Nestled on the Chigamane estate in Vilanculos, Mozambique, our small farm is dedicated to providing healthy and pesticide-free produce to our community and neighbouring lodges. Our commitment to education and food security extends beyond our own farm, as we work to empower our neighbours through community education and agricultural knowledge. Join us on our journey to a tastier and more sustainable future.

Our saplings at MozFruit are nurtured with all-natural, pesticide-free methods, including composting and reducing food waste from the estate, resulting in healthy and delicious fruits, as you can taste.

Dragon fruit flowers are a unique and special crop, blooming for one night only. Our team works in harmony with nature to ensure maximum yield by pollinating the flowers at the optimal time.

Our careful farming practices result in large and sweet fruits, as seen on the scale. We closely monitor sugar content and track crop progress to ensure optimal yield and soil health.

The Ruby Red dragonfruit at MozFruit boasts a vibrant red interior that is as sweet as it is visually striking. Indulge in its juicy sweetness, a taste that will linger fondly in your memory.

Our team at MozFruit specializes in dragon fruit and has expertly grafted various varieties of mangoes to create a unique and delicious flavor exclusive to our farm.

Visit us at MozFruit and enjoy a bountiful selection of mangoes, passionfruit, papaya, and bananas, freshly picked and waiting in our fruit bowls.

We believe in giving back to our community and providing special care packages to nearby lodges and local schools. We take pride in MozFruit being a good neighbour.

Chili is a staple in Mozambican cuisine and at MozFruit, we dry and crush our chilis into powder, adding flavor and heat to dishes. Our chili bushes also attract a diverse bird population to the farm.

Tomatoes are aplenty at MozFruit, not just in our cultivated gardens but also growing wild on trees. A result of birds helping themselves to our first harvest, now available for everyone to enjoy.
